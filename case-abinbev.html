Creative Brand Grand Prix

ABINBEV

Creativity at Scale

Ogilvy 2026

The Challenge

In a world where consumers are becoming more distant from brands, how do you create meaningful connections at scale>

The Idea

Creativity at Scale was born from the insight that true creativity isn't about one big moment, but about consistently delivering impactful ideas across multiple touchpoints, markets, and cultures.

The Result

A global platform that enabled AB InBeV to deliver personalized, culturally relevant content across 50+ countries, driving measurable growth in brand effectiveness and consumer engagement.

Key Learnings

  • Scalability doesn't mean repetition: Each market needs local relevance.
  • Data-driven creativity: Use insights to fuel personalization, not limit it.
  • Consistency is key: One big idea, adapted locally.
  • Measure what matters: Brand health and business outcomes, not just likes.
